Our Programs

ORGANISING CAMPS

We organize blood donation camps in various cities with various government blood banks. We believe that organizing camps is the fastest way to solving the problem of blood shortage. They are safe, and give you a very easy way out to donate blood (saves a trip to the hospital). Raktarpan aims to increase voluntary blood donation through these camps

AWARENESS SESSIONS

We organize various awareness activities: street plays, competitions, sessions, etc. All the activities aim to inspire people to come forward to donate blood. They have a long term impact in spreading the idea of blood donation and clearing myths. These sessions provide a mature understanding of the blood shortage in India and motivates people to live for a cause.

Click to organise an awareness session.

RUNNING HELPLINE

With a sole aim of helping people in dire need of blood, we run a 24x7 blood helpline. It is a short term solution we provide, till the time we are unable to achieve zero shortage. It is run by college students who not only handle the request, but also build the donor database. It is for the cases when somebody needs blood and is not able to arrange a donor
